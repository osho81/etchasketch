Press arrow keys to draw, e.g. press to draw a line upwards.

For diagonal line, press two pertinent directions, e.g. & is diagonally up to the left.

Clear drawing board by pressing the key space or c.

Change color during drawing: r = red, b = blue, g = green, d = default black.

Change line thickness during drawing: 4 = 4 pixels, 8 = 8 pixels, 1 = default 1 pixel.
